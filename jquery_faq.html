Q:What is the highest peak in the park? A:Eagle Peak in the southeastern part of Yellowstone is the highest at 11,358 feet.
Grand Canyon National Park
Q:How big is it? A:That depends on how you look at it. The park includes over a million acres of land - 1,218,375.54 acres / 493,077 hectares, to be exact, or 1,904 square miles / 4931 square kilometers.
Yosemite National Park
Q:Where can I see giant sequoias? A:There are three giant sequoia groves in Yosemite National Park: the Mariposa Grove, the Tuolumne Grove, and the Merced Grove.
Zion National Park
Q:Can I bicycle into Zion Canyon? A:. Bicycles are allowed on the Zion Canyon Scenic Drive. Due to safety concerns cyclists are required to pull over and stop when approached from behind by a shuttle bus. Do not pass a moving shuttle bus.
