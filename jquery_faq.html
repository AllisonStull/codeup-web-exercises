Arches

  • There are more than 2000 natural sandstone arches inside Arches National Park.
  • Landscape Arch has the longest span of any arch in North America, 306 feet (93.3 meters), and is the fourth longest in the world.
  • The most famous natural stone arch in the world - Delicate Arch! Known from photos, this arch has become iconic due to its freestanding shape.
  • The enormous sandstone boulder atop Balanced Rock is presumed to weigh approximately 3,577 tons!

White Sands(not a national park)

  • White Sands is almost pure gypsum
  • Gypsum is actually a clear substance; the dunes appear white like snow because the gypsum grains are constantly banging into each other. The scratches then reflect the sun’s rays making them appear white.
  • Gypsum doesn’t absorb heat from the sun. So even on the hottest day of the year, the dunes are cool and comfortable to walk on.
  • The sand sparkles!!!

Yellowstone

  • Half the world’s hydrothermal features are found at Yellowstone.
  • Yellowstone is a supervolcano.
  • Yellowstone is home to the largest concentration of mammals in the lower 48.
  • Yellowstone has its own Grand Canyon.
